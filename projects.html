Indoor Outdoor Detection Leaderboard

Research

This was the first project I worked on as an Undergraduate Researcher at Professor Ali Abedi's research group at UW–Madison. We wrote a paper on this project, which was accepted for IEEE DySPAN 2026. It was also used in a competition run by SpectrumX.

The site lets users upload their machine learning environment-sensing models and tests/compares them against each other, encouraging frictionless reproducibility. I also collected numerous datasets of airplane ADS-B signals for users to train and test their models on.
